Core Competitions

Flagship challenges across robotics, AI, and human augmentation design.

Robowar 2026

Build a combat robot, survive the arena. Battle-hardened machines meet under IIT Bombay's floodlights in the ultimate test of engineering and strategy.

Neural Hackathon
